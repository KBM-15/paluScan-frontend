Protect Yourself from Malaria

🛌🦟

Sleep Under Mosquito Nets

Use insecticide-treated nets (ITNs) every night, even during dry seasons. Properly tuck nets under mattress.

🏠💨

Indoor Residual Spraying

Apply WHO-approved insecticides on walls & ceilings where mosquitoes rest. Effective for 3-6 months.

🧴🦵

Use Mosquito Repellent

Apply DEET, picaridin, or oil of lemon eucalyptus on exposed skin, especially at dusk and dawn.

🌿🚫

Eliminate Standing Water

Empty flower pots, tires, and containers weekly. Mosquitoes breed in stagnant water within 7-10 days.

👕🧥

Wear Protective Clothing

Long sleeves, pants, and socks during peak mosquito hours (sunset to sunrise). Light colors attract fewer mosquitoes.

💊🏥

Seek Early Treatment

Fever + chills? Get tested immediately. Early diagnosis with PaluScan saves lives.

🌍 WHO Recommendation: Combine multiple prevention methods for best protection. Early diagnosis and treatment are critical.
